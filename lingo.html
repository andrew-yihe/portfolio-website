Lingo Racer

Crowdsourcing system UI design

2020

About

Lingo Racer is a crowdsourcing social application designed as a game to be launched in tablet and PC platforms. The key objective of Lingo Racer is to translate digital books and documents in a library from one language to another using the collective contributions of the users. The design of the app requires two types of users to collaborate at the same time to achieve the above objective – “Translator” and “Proof-reader” - thus making it a “collocative” and “synchronous” crowdsourcing application.

This project is a collaborative effort focusing on the high-fidelity prototype design. As team leader, I was responsible for initiating the ideas of the project, concept sketching, wireframes prototyping and the final high-fidelity model prototyping.
