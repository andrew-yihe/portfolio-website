The Lighthouse

VR interactive environment design

2019

About

The lighthouse is an explorable VR experience made with Unity Virtual Reality Toolkit (VRTK). The scene is set inside a lighthouse by the sea on a rainy night, underpinned by a gentle touch of a piano soundtrack.

All objects in the scene have physics, allowing player to interact with the environment using the VR controller, for example, playing a phonograph, lighting a fire in the fireplace, opening a mystery box, etc.

This is an individual project. I consider this development process as a perfect opportunity for practicing my problem-solving skills, notably when being introduced a new tool in a short-term project.

Development
Tools

AR development – Unity 3D
Light sensor implementation – Arduino
3D modelling - Autodesk Maya
Texture design - Adobe Photoshop
Music and sound editing - Logic Pro X
Demo video composing – Adobe Premiere Pro
