The Adventures of Elliot

AR educational storybook

2019

About

The Adventures of Elliot is an interactive story book fused with innovative technologies. With various AR interactions throughout the story, readers will find themselves immersed into the story as Elliot, who will be asked by the king to go thorough different levels in order to save the forest kingdom. Combining appealing stories with AR, our goal is to experiment and challenge the ways of reading.

Our target demographic is mainly focused on kids around 6 to 8 years old, by providing them a platform to fully experience AR technology as well as practicing the sense of music, the ability of problem-solving and enthusiasm in reading.

This project is a collaborative effort. As team leader, I was responsible for the pre-planning of the project, 3D modelling, AR implementation, sound design and final presentation.

Development
